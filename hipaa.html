The ePHI of Your Clients is Our Priority

Safegaurding electronic Protected Health Information

HIPAA is United States’ legislation that provides data privacy and security provisions for safeguarding medical information. Perfect Day Reminders has worked hard to make sure the electronic Protected Heath Information of your clients is secure.

The Steps We Take to Secure Your Account


  • Secure Socket Layers (SSL) to transmit all data

  • HIPAA Compliant Databases and Servers

  • Encrypted data storage using industry standard AES-256 database encryption keys

  • All user names and passwords stored in our database are encrypted

  • HIPAA Accounts are limited to one provider to monitor changes to account

  • Automatic Log Off after a predetermined time of inactivity

  • Frozen Accounts after multiple failed attempts

  • Signed BAA (Business Associate Agreement) between Protected Entity and Business Associate
